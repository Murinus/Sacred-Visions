Sacred Visions was formed in 2019 and are based in the Oakboro / Charlotte, NC area.
Sacred Visions is comprised of a group of hardworking, dedicated and humbled musicians with a vision to bring a different musical expierence to the game.

Blending different genres in hopes to open peoples hearts and minds to what is real and important in life. God, Love, Peace and Oneness taking listeners through many realms of consciousness with Metal!
